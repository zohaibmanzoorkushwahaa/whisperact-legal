How it works

Three steps, zero effort

From spoken thought to structured action in seconds.

01

Tap and speak

Hit the mic. Talk freely — tasks, reminders, events, or just notes. A live transcript streams as you speak.

02

AI extracts structure

Cloud AI, Apple Intelligence, and a local smart parser work in cascade — so you always get results, even offline.

03

Synced to Apple apps

Tasks land in Apple Reminders. Events land in Apple Calendar. Everything editable right where you already work.

Features

Built for the way you think

Every feature starts from the same question: what should happen automatically?

13 recording languages

English, Urdu, Arabic, Spanish, French, Portuguese, German, Turkish, Russian, Indonesian, Italian, Korean, Japanese. Switch right on the recording screen. Tasks always come out in English regardless of what language you recorded in.

Fully offline capable

Captures, transcribes, and extracts on-device for English. Items saved locally and pushed when you reconnect.

Upcoming tab

Every timed item across all memos in one place — Overdue, Today, Tomorrow, This Week, Later.

Times & places captured

Say "lunch at Dolmen Mall from 1 to 2" — WhisperAct writes the event with location and exact duration.

Smart notifications

Every reminder alerts you right at its due time, with an optional earlier heads-up so nothing slips — free for everyone. Events carry their own lead-time alert. Add a Daily Digest for a morning briefing, or Daily Review to triage what still needs a date.

Pro

iCloud sync

Your full memo library across all your iPhones and iPads. No WhisperAct account — data stays in your iCloud.

Pro

Home & Lock Screen widgets

See today's agenda, what's due, and your overdue count at a glance — right on your Home Screen and Lock Screen, updated through the day.

Re-Analyze

Recorded offline and got basic results? One tap re-runs cloud AI on the saved transcript — no re-recording. Free includes 7 a month; Pro is unlimited.

Daily Digest

One notification at your chosen time — a time-ordered rundown of today's events, reminders, and overdue items. Free for everyone.

Share & export

Plain text and Markdown sharing free for everyone. Pro unlocks PDF export and bulk multi-memo export.

Privacy

Private by design, not policy

For English on iOS 26 devices, transcription runs entirely on-device — your audio never leaves your phone. Non-English audio is sent to Groq's Whisper API and not retained after processing. No WhisperAct account. No analytics. No ads. Your data lives in your iCloud, not ours.
