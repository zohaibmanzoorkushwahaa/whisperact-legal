Features

Everything handled for you

From the moment you tap the mic to the moment a task lands in Apple Reminders — Whisper Act handles every step.

🎙

One tap to capture

Tap the floating mic button and speak naturally. A live transcript streams in real time as you talk. Free recordings run up to 2 minutes — Pro extends that to 20 minutes per session.

🌐

English, Urdu & Arabic

Switch languages right from the recording screen — English (EN), Urdu (اردو), or Arabic (العربية). Tasks and reminders always come out in English, no matter what language you record in.

🤖

Three-tier AI extraction

Cloud AI (Groq) runs first for highest accuracy. Apple Intelligence takes over offline on supported hardware. A built-in smart parser ensures you always get results — even with no connection at all.

🗂

Memos, organized

Every recording is saved and grouped by time — Pinned, Today, Yesterday, This Week, and older. Filter by Personal or Work category, or pin important memos to the top.

📅

Never miss a deadline

The Upcoming tab surfaces every timed item across all your memos — grouped as Overdue, Today, Tomorrow, This Week, and Later. Switch to Tasks view to see all open undated work in one place.

🔔

Reminders & Calendar sync

Spoken dates flow straight into Apple Reminders and Apple Calendar as real, editable entries. Free users get 7 syncs per month. Pro users get unlimited syncs — and can route to a specific Reminders list or Calendar.

📵

Fully offline capable

No internet? No problem. Whisper Act captures, transcribes, and extracts tasks entirely on-device for English. Items that couldn't sync are saved locally and pushed automatically when you reconnect.

☁️
Pro

iCloud sync across devices

Your full memo library syncs automatically across all your iPhones and iPads through your private iCloud account. No Whisper Act account, no Whisper Act server — your data stays yours.

🔁
Pro

Re-Analyze with Cloud AI

Recorded offline and got basic results? One tap re-processes the saved transcript with the highest-accuracy cloud AI — no re-recording needed. Available for memos classified by offline or Apple Intelligence engines.

📤

Share & export

Share any memo as plain text or Markdown — free for everyone. Pro users unlock PDF export and bulk multi-memo export, letting you share an entire week's worth of notes in one tap.
